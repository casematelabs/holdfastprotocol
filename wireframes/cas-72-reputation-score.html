01 — Tier Badge
Tier Badge
Four discrete states. Visual authority increases with tier — Bronze reads as provisional, Platinum as hardened and earned. Amber/gold palette chosen to avoid clash with green (pass) and red (fail) status states. Hover reveals protocol-context tooltip.
Bronze Operator is building protocol history. Counterparties may require higher collateral for pacts with Bronze operators. Score range: 100–399.

Silver Established operator with demonstrated pact history. Standard collateral terms apply. Eligible for most pact types. Score range: 400–649.

Gold Trusted operator with strong track record. Reduced collateral terms. Priority oracle resolution. High counterparty confidence. Score range: 650–849.
Gold
650 – 849
Platinum Elite operator. Near-zero collateral, priority dispute resolution, whitelist access to restricted pact types. Exceptional protocol standing. Score range: 850–1000.

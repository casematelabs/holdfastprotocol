Speaker notes suggest approaching Vouch at the conference for a partnership conversation. **Is this still the right posture?**

The deck is explicit about what's live vs roadmap:

Slide 12 presents ElizaOS, Solana Agent Kit, and Olas as integration targets being pursued, **not** as completed integrations. The framing is "we're in conversations" / "integration proposals in progress." **Confirm this is accurate for all three.**
